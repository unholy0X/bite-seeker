05 / 12
User experience
Built for
a kitchen,
not a laptop.
Every screen is a bottom sheet, swipe gesture, or full-bleed card. There is no hamburger menu. There is no desktop layout squeezed onto a phone.
Wallet auth
Replaces the login form. One tap. Sign with Phantom. No email. No password. No account creation.
Camera extraction
Snap up to 3 photos of a cookbook page. AI reads ingredients, quantities, and steps — including handwritten recipes.
Offline-resilient
Zustand + SecureStore keep the full pantry and shopping list accessible with no signal — useful in actual supermarkets.
RTL + i18n
Full support for English, French, and Arabic with forced RTL layout. Recipes extracted in the user's own language.
No web version. No PWA. No Electron wrapper. MWA requires a physical Android device — this is not portable to a browser.

Roadmap — creator economy
The app becomes a platform.
Chefs earn. Holders benefit.
Every SKR earned by a creator is paid by a user — no platform cut, no middleman. The protocol is the business model.
Now → Month 1
Ship & distribute
  • Solana dApp Store listing
  • Public recipe sharing — any user can publish an extracted recipe
  • SKR Pro tier visible in Seeker wallet aggregators
Month 2–3
Creator accounts
  • Verified chef profiles — link wallet, set creator handle
  • Publish gated recipe packs: free tier previews, full content behind SKR paywall
  • Save-to-earn: every time a user saves a creator's recipe, creator earns a micro SKR cut
  • SKR-weighted community ranking — top recipes rise by holder votes
Month 4–6
Monetisation layer
  • Creator SKR subscriptions — fans pay monthly in SKR for exclusive drops
  • Tip in SKR directly from any recipe card — one tap, MWA signs
  • Co-authored recipes — split royalties on-chain, no escrow, no trust
  • 50% burn on all creator tx fees — every creator sale reduces SKR supply
Long term
Full marketplace
  • Creator storefronts: meal plans, diet programs, full cookbooks — priced in SKR
  • Brand sponsorships settled on-chain — ingredient sponsors pay creators in SKR
  • Grocery delivery API: shopping list → cart in one tap, affiliate revenue in SKR
  • Seeker device NFC → instant pantry scan at checkout
Every creator transaction burns 50% of the SKR fee — the bigger the creator economy grows, the faster the supply contracts.
